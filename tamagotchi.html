Welcome to Bootleg Tamagotchi!

How To Play:

  • Clean Pet: Keep your pet clean by clicking the "Clean" button.
  • Feed Pet: Satisfy your pet's hunger by clicking the "Feed" button.
  • Play with Pet: Make your pet happy by clicking the "Play" button.
  • Put to Sleep: Ensure your pet gets enough rest by clicking the "Sleep" button.

Be sure to check in on your pet regularly to keep it healthy and happy!
